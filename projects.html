AQUIFER

Role: Motor Systems Lead

"Aqueous, quick-charging battery integration for electric flight research" combines a nano-electric fluid aqueous flow battery that eliminates the fire and explosion hazards of conventional batteries and fuel cells with a rim-driven motor to reduce weight, noise and charge time. The rim-driven motor concept could enable motors that are more efficient both electrically and aerodynamically. I served as motor lead, responsible for modeling, prototyping, and techinical monitor for a subcontracter.
